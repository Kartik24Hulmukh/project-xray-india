# Monday announcement draft

## Headline

Project X-Ray India opens a controlled synthetic technical preview for evidence-workflow evaluation

## Body

Project X-Ray India is an open-source reference implementation for creating source-anchored, independently reviewed, and correctable public-infrastructure evidence dossiers.

This week we are opening a **controlled synthetic technical preview** to invited researchers, journalists, public-interest technologists, and engineers. The demonstration uses entirely synthetic people, organizations, and documents. It is designed to test whether the workflow is understandable, reproducible, and resistant to premature publication.

It is **not** a voter-facing election product, a corruption detector, a candidate database, or a certified production deployment. Operator production gates remain open in the readiness ledger. We are not claiming users, partners, impact, or traction.

### Call to action

Reproduce the synthetic workflow and tell us where the evidence chain, review process, privacy boundary, or documentation breaks.

### Available

- Synthetic walkthrough / local quickstart
- Architecture and safety overview
- Known limitations
- Reproduction kit
- Security reporting channel

### Not available

- Real-person or real-project dossiers
- Production certification
- Unsupervised publication
- Partner/user/impact claims
