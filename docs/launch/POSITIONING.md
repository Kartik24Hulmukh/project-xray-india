## Value / impact / traction distinction

- **Value:** usefulness of the workflow and evidence invariants.
- **Impact:** real outcomes such as disclosures, corrections, reviews, repairs — not claimed yet.
- **Traction:** repeated real use by independent teams — not claimed yet.

You may launch high-potential value. You may not honestly declare high impact or high traction before measured use.
