- Freeze synthetic preview package
- Complete AWS account security
- Gumloop plan-only infra
- Hosted or local synthetic preview
- Invited evaluators only

## Days 3–14

- Fix onboarding/safety issues from evaluators
- Complete target MFA, restore, alert receipts if hosting
- 10–20 discovery interviews with journalists, RTI groups, auditors, engineers
- No real publication

## Days 15–30

- Two historical/synthetic deep dossiers under full review discipline
- Measure preparation-time and exact-source acceptance
- Draft counsel-reviewed real-case policy pack

## Days 31–60

- Up to three controlled pilots
- Manual intake only
- No automatic public allegations
- Named editorial/privacy/security owners required

## Days 61–90

- Require repeat workflow from at least two teams
- One measurable outcome or serious funded pilot discussion
- Kill or narrow scope if safety or demand fails

## Kill criteria

- Unsupported publication
- Repeated privacy leakage
- No independent reproductions
- No professional demand after honest evaluation
- Legal/political containment failure
