# Privacy, retention, correction and takedown draft

**Status:** unsigned draft. Named privacy/legal owners must approve before real data use.

## Principles

1. Private by default.
2. Public output is allowlisted, not denylisted.
3. Candidate/private/reviewer/storage fields never appear anonymously.
4. Corrections preserve history; withdrawals leave safe tombstones.
5. Real sensitive personal data (Aadhaar, PAN, home address, personal phone, bank details, signatures) must not be published.

## Preview policy

- Synthetic data only.
- No public self-serve intake of real evidence.
- Retention of preview logs should be short and access-controlled.

## Correction channel

Operators should publish:

- privacy contact;
- correction contact;
- security contact.

Until real contacts exist, keep real publication disabled.

## Takedown / suppression

Emergency privacy or legal suppression may hide substantive public content immediately, but must:

- create an audit event;
- leave a public-safe tombstone where policy requires;
- escalate to the named legal/privacy owner.

## Right of reply

Any future real named allegation requires a meaningful opportunity for subject/authority response according to operator counsel and applicable norms. This preview does not publish real named allegations.
