# India controlled-preview legal and political gates — July 2026

**Risk assessment, not legal advice. Qualified Indian media, privacy and technology counsel must review any real-person use.**

## Monday boundary

- Invitation-only and synthetic-only.
- No public uploads, tips, comments or arbitrary URL fetching.
- No real people, organisations, allegations, case numbers or copied real narratives.
- No search indexing or public dossier endpoints.
- No rankings, integrity scores, guilt/corruption labels or partisan targeting.
- No promise of anonymity, privilege, whistleblower protection or legal compliance.

## Principal risk areas

- India retains criminal defamation under BNS section 356; truth/public good and good-faith exceptions are fact-sensitive: https://www.mha.gov.in/sites/default/files/250883_english_01042024.pdf
- DPDP Rules 2025 were notified with phased commencement; build privacy, notice, security, rights and breach processes now: https://www.meity.gov.in/documents/act-and-policies/digital-personal-data-protection-rules-2025-gDOxUjMtQWa?pageTitle=Digital-Personal-Data-Protection-Rules-2025
- Current IT Rules and takedown obligations require counsel to classify the operator as publisher, intermediary or both: https://www.meity.gov.in/static/uploads/2026/02/550681ab908f8afb135b0ad42816a1c9.pdf
- Publication affecting pending proceedings can create contempt risk: https://www.indiacode.nic.in/bitstream/123456789/1514/1/A1971-70.pdf
- Copyright fair dealing is purpose-specific; government works are not automatically unrestricted: https://www.indiacode.nic.in/show-data?actid=AC_CEN_9_30_00006_195714_1517807321712&sectionId=14572&sectionno=52&orderno=70
- CERT-In directions can require six-hour reporting and 180-day log retention for covered incidents: https://www.cert-in.org.in/PDF/CERT-In_Directions_70B_28.04.2022.pdf

## Real-case hard gate

Any real-person or real-case workflow is a separate launch requiring written Indian counsel review, source rights clearance, privacy impact assessment, public-interest/necessity record, right of response, contempt/election review where relevant, protected-identity screening, legal/privacy/editorial approvals, tested correction/takedown/appeal, named accountable owners and insurance/entity review.

## Required wording

Project X-Ray is an experimental evidence-workflow tool. A citation proves only that a source contains the anchored material; it does not establish that an allegation is true. The project does not determine guilt, detect corruption, replace legal/editorial judgment or guarantee source truth.
