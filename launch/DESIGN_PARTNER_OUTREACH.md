# Design-partner outreach — the highest-leverage launch activity

Pre-agreed traction gate: **3 design partners in 30 days or narrow/stop** [VF].
Target list (build 20 names Mon/Tue): investigative/data journalists,
RTI practitioners' groups, accountability NGOs, journalism-school data labs,
civic-tech collectives (e.g. groups active around open contracting / OCDS
data in the launch region), public-procurement researchers.

## Template A — journalist / data-journalism team
Subject: A disciplined evidence pipeline for tender/RTI documents — 15 min?

Hi [name] — I read your work on [specific story]. I've open-sourced a small
evidence-workflow engine for accountability documents: quarantined intake,
source anchors on every claim, mandatory two-person review before anything
becomes public, tamper-evident audit history. It deliberately does NOT score
corruption or verify truth — it enforces process.

I'm looking for 3 design partners to shape it against real workflows (using
sanitized/public documents only). Would a 15-minute call this week be useful?
Repo: [link]. No pitch beyond this — if it's not useful, telling me why would
also help enormously.

## Template B — NGO / RTI group
Subject: Two-person review + audit trail for document-based casework

Hi [name] — organizations doing RTI/procurement work often track document
evidence in folders and spreadsheets, and errors are costly. I've built an
open-source workflow tool that enforces: nothing public without two reviewer
approvals, every claim anchored to a source, every action in an immutable
audit log, corrections tracked visibly. Free, self-hostable, works offline.
Could I show it to whoever runs casework, for 15 minutes? I'm explicitly
seeking critique from practitioners before opening it further.

## Template C — engineer / potential reviewer
Subject: 2-hour security review of a small stdlib-Python codebase?

Hi [name] — I'm launching an open-source evidence-workflow engine (pure
stdlib Python, ~1,900 LOC, 130 tests) and I refuse to ship security claims
without an independent human review. Would you spend 2 hours reviewing the
auth gateway + audit chain? I'll credit you in the release notes (or keep it
private — your call), and I'll return the favor on your project.

## Qualification checklist (a "design partner" must…)
- [ ] Actually handle accountability documents (tenders/RTI/audits) in their work
- [ ] Agree to a recurring 30-min feedback session for 4 weeks
- [ ] Be willing to test with a sanitized/public document pack
- [ ] Understand and accept the synthetic-first, no-real-allegations preview scope
Anything less is an audience member, not a partner. Do not count them.
