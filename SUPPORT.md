# Support

Tracepaper is maintained by a solo maintainer during the controlled beta.

- Security reports: see SECURITY.md (please use private disclosure).
- Bugs/feature requests: GitHub issues. Triage happens in batches; expect a
  response within 3-5 days, not hours.
- Grievance, correction, or takedown requests about published content:
  see legal/GRIEVANCE_AND_TAKEDOWN.md (acknowledged within 24 hours).
- During the controlled beta only synthetic/approved sanitized documents are
  in scope. Real-person allegations are out of scope by policy.
